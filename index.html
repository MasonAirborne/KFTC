FAQ

Answer the questions that stop people from booking.

Do I need any experience to do a Discovery Flight?

No. It’s designed for first-timers. We’ll walk you through everything step by step.

How long does it take to get a Private Pilot License?

It depends on your schedule and consistency. Most students progress faster with regular weekly lessons.

What should I bring?

ID, sunglasses, closed-toe shoes, and a great attitude. We’ll handle the rest.
